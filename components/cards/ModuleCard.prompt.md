Platform-module tile used in feature grids — round green icon, serif title, short description, green link with arrow. Hover lifts + glows.

```jsx
<ModuleCard
  icon={<MapIcon />}
  title="Fazendas e talhões"
  description="Mapa, áreas, culturas, safras e histórico por propriedade."
  linkLabel="Explorar módulo"
/>
```

Lay out four across in a responsive grid. Keep descriptions to one line of context.
